How It Works
  • System Installation
    The solar system is installed on-site at no upfront cost.
  • Energy Generation
    The system generates energy that is used on-site, reducing grid consumption.
  • Pay for Solar Energy
    You will receive two bills: one from Agile Energy for solar generation (cents/kWh), and one from your existing retailer for the remaining grid electricity.
  • Fully Managed Solution
    Agile Energy manages all monitoring, maintenance, insurance, and system performance.
