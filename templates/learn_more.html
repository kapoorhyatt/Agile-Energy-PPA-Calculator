Our Solution

Agile Energy delivers fully funded behind-the-meter, on-site solar and battery assets under an off-balance-sheet structure, requiring no upfront capital.

Customers secure energy at 20–40% below grid rates through a low-risk model with contracted performance, predictable pricing and full lifecycle asset management provided by Agile.

  • Off balance sheet lending
  • Landlord management for leased properties
  • Uplift NABERS ratings and reduce carbon footprint

Benefits of a PPA

Long-Term, Predictable Revenue & Pricing

Enjoy stable electricity costs and reliable energy supply over the life of the PPA.

No Upfront Capital Required

Access solar energy without any initial investment while avoiding asset management responsibilities.

Flexible Terms

Agreements can be tailored to your business needs and future growth, providing maximum flexibility.

No Cost for Monitoring, Replacements or Maintenance

Agile Energy covers all ongoing system upkeep at no cost to you.

No Insurance Cost for Solar Asset

The solar system is fully insured by Agile Energy—no insurance cost for your business.

Option to Add Storage or Expand

Flexibility to upgrade or expand your system as your energy needs grow.

Minimum Internal Resourcing Requirement

Systems are managed by Agile, so your team can focus elsewhere.

Buyout Anytime

Buyout options during or after term at pre-agreed prices. Full ownership at PPA completion.
